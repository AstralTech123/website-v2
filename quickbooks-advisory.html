How it works

We begin with a full assessment of your current accounting setup — whether you're starting fresh, migrating from Desktop to QBO, or switching from another platform entirely. We handle the entire setup and migration process, ensuring your historical data comes over clean and your chart of accounts is structured for the way you actually run your business.

From there, we optimize your chart of accounts for clear, accurate reporting, then connect your third-party integrations — payroll, inventory, point-of-sale, e-commerce — so everything flows into QuickBooks automatically. The result is a streamlined ecosystem where your financial data is always current and always reliable.
